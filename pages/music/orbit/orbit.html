>Orbit

Album Background/Info

Orbit is my 2nd full length album. Like An End of Sorts this album has come together over a large period of time and with an overarching "story" behind it. I think it should be obvious by now that I love space, the stars, and anything like it that has that cosmic feel. Alongside that, I wanted this album's cover art to reflect the new direction I decided to go with my music this time around, mimicking an extremely old-video-game feel with low resolution graphics and low-poly models

The album is a progression throughout one whole orbit around some unknown star startng in the early morning, and eventually leading into the dead of night. There's a little substory somewhere in there that deals with the inhabitants of this world, who seem to live a feudal lifestyle, and practice a religeon through the sky.

At the time of writing this, the LP has been in production for just about a year counting some of the older songs on the current tracklist. Though that's not saying much as the tracklist keeps chaning so much the album could span 3 years or 2 months.
